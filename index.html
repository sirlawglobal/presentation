🎥 Stateless Media Streaming

The Buffer-Pipe Strategy

We use streamifier to bridge the gap between incoming request buffers and the Cloudinary SDK.

  • ✅ Zero Local Storage: Files never touch the backend's hard drive.
  • ✅ Max Throughput: Optimized for large video uploads (up to 100MB).
  • ✅ Security: All uploads are verified for MIME-type and role permissions.
